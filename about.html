Parish Information

Welcome to Our Lady of Perpetual Help Catholic Church, affectionately known as "The Church on The Hill." We are a Roman Catholic Community serving the Black Catholics of Washington DC for more than 100 years! Our rich cultural legacy and vibrant faith community make us a unique part of the Anacostia neighborhood.

Our parish office is open Monday through Thursday from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM. We welcome visitors and new parishioners to join us for Mass and participate in our various ministries and community outreach programs.
